Friendliest City

Galway has been recognized as Europe's friendliest city multiple times, most recently in 2020.

Claddagh Ring Origin

The iconic Claddagh Ring, symbolizing love, loyalty, and friendship with its design of two hands holding a heart topped with a crown, originated in Galway.

Cultural Vibrancy

Known as the "Cultural Heart of Ireland," Galway hosts approximately 123 festivals annually, averaging more than two festivals per week.

Festive Celebrations

The Galway Continental Christmas Market, held annually in Eyre Square, is Ireland's top-rated Christmas market, featuring over 50 wooden chalets, amusement rides, and festive food stalls.
